Webpage to explain the process of Salesforecasing on one of Kaggle Competition:
 -Walmart Weekly Sales Forecasting
 -Competition of kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/

It shows line charts of historical data and preducted result.

Users can choose a store and a department which they want to see.

Data files are generated from another repository: https://github.com/SsureyMoon/Walmart-Forecasting-NN
